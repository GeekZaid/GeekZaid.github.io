This is the dodge viper. It was produced from 1992 to 2017 by Dodge (and briefly by SRT). The Viper is known for its powerful engine, bold styling, and high performance. It was conceived as a modern take on the classic Shelby Cobra, with a focus on a raw and unfiltered driving experience. The car was initially equipped with an 8.0-liter V10 engine, developed with input from Lamborghini. Later models featured larger engines and more horsepower, with the final version boasting 645 hp. The Viper had several generations, each with design and mechanical updates. It gained recognition in racing, including victories at the 24 Hours of Le Mans. Production took place at the Conner Avenue Assembly Plant in Detroit.
